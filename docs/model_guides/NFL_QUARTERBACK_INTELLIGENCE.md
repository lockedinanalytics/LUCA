# NFL Quarterback Intelligence - Epic 2A

## Purpose
Epic 2A introduces NFL Quarterback Intelligence as the football equivalent of MLB Starting Pitcher Intelligence.

## Components
- Passing Efficiency
- Explosive Passing
- Pressure Resilience
- Decision Quality
- Mobility
- Situational Performance
- Matchup Fit

## Output
The engine returns:
- passing_efficiency_score
- explosive_passing_score
- pressure_resilience_score
- decision_quality_score
- mobility_score
- situational_score
- matchup_score
- ceiling_score
- floor_score
- volatility_score
- final_qb_score
- confidence
- warnings
- explainability
