# NFL Market Intelligence & Unified Decision Engine - Epic 2E

## Purpose
Epic 2E connects NFL intelligence modules into a unified decision engine.

## Components
- NFL simulation
- Market intelligence wrapper
- Unified NFL decision input/output
- Moneyline/spread/total probability support
- EV and unit suggestion
- Explainability

## Output
The engine returns:
- luca_score
- projected_probability
- confidence
- expected_value
- recommendation_tier
- suggested_units
- simulation
- explainability
- warnings

## Sample endpoints
- `/decision/nfl/simulation/sample`
- `/decision/nfl/unified/sample`
